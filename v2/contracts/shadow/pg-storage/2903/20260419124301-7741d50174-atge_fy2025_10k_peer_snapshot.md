Snapshot notes:

- Adtalem describes itself as a leading U.S. healthcare educator and says it operates five institutions focused on healthcare programs.
- Adtalem says it serves more than 90,000 students across campuses and online and has an alumni community of about 350,000.
- The company says it is focused on expanding access, using learning technology, and aligning programs with workforce demand.
- Adtalem reports three segments, including Chamberlain and Walden, illustrating a different but still relevant private higher-education operating model.

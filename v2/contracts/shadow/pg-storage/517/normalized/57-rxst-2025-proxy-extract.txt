Key facts extracted for Data Collection:
- The proxy identifies four named executive officers for 2024: Ron Kurtz (President and CEO), Shelley Thunen (Co-President and CFO), Eric Weinberg (Co-President and Chief Commercial Officer), and Ilya Goldshleger (Co-President and Chief Operating Officer).
- The proxy includes executive compensation, governance, board, and committee information.
- Compensation section says Ron Kurtz's annual base salary was increased 6.2% to $685,000 effective March 1, 2024.
- Other named executive officers had annual base salaries increased 5.4% to $485,000 effective March 1, 2024.
- The proxy's business overview repeats the core product description: LAL/LAL+ plus LDD, and the positioning as the only commercially available premium cataract technology with postoperative adjustability.
- 2024 business highlights in the proxy include 98,055 LALs sold in 2024 and an installed base of 971 LDDs at year-end 2024.

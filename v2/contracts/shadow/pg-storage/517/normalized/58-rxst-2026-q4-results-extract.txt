Key facts extracted for Data Collection:
- Title: "RxSight, Inc. Reports Fourth Quarter and 2025 Results; Issues 2026 Guidance."
- 2025 Light Adjustable Lens unit sales increased 12% to 109,615.
- Over 300,000 RxSight LAL procedures had been performed since launch.
- LDD installed base expanded to 1,134.
- Regulatory approval was received in Australia.
- Full-year 2025 sales were $134.5 million, down 4% year over year; LAL revenue grew 12% while LDD system sales declined 48%.
- 2025 gross profit was $103.0 million, or 76.6% of revenue.
- 2025 operating expenses were $151.2 million.
- 2026 guidance called for revenue of $120.0 million to $135.0 million, gross margin of 70% to 72%, and operating expenses of $150.0 million to $160.0 million.

# Snapshot

Snapshot date: 2026-04-19
Source date: Filing date 2026-03-02; period ended 2025-12-31

Key extracted points:

- Lincoln Educational Services Corporation trades under ticker `LINC` on the Nasdaq Stock Market LLC.
- The company provides career-oriented postsecondary education to recent high school graduates and working adults.
- It operated 22 campuses in 12 states as of December 31, 2025.
- Brands include Lincoln Technical Institute, Lincoln College of Technology, and Nashville Auto Diesel College.
- Program areas include skilled trades, automotive, health sciences, and information technology.
- As of December 31, 2025, the company had 17,046 students enrolled and average enrollment of 16,622 for FY2025.
- Annual revenue for FY2025 was $518.2 million, up 17.8% from the prior year.
- The company reports two segments: Campus Operations and Transitional.
- No campuses were classified in the Transitional segment as of December 31, 2025.
- The company had $28.5 million of cash and cash equivalents as of December 31, 2025.
- It had no debt outstanding under its revolving credit facility as of December 31, 2025.
- The filing highlights regulatory exposure tied to accreditation, Title IV funding, the 90/10 rule, cohort default rates, and broader education-sector compliance.

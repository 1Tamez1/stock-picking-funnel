# Snapshot

Snapshot date: 2026-04-19
Source date: Filing date 2026-04-16; event date 2026-04-13

Key extracted points:

- Lincoln entered into an amended and restated credit agreement on April 13, 2026.
- The new revolving credit facility increased maximum principal capacity from $60 million to $125 million.
- The facility includes a $10 million letter-of-credit sublimit and a $25 million accordion feature.
- The stated uses are working capital, general corporate purposes, and other permitted purposes.
- The facility is secured by a first-priority lien on substantially all personal property of the company and its subsidiaries and is guaranteed by wholly owned subsidiaries.
- The agreement matures on April 11, 2031.
- Pricing is SOFR- or base-rate-based plus an applicable margin that steps with leverage.
- The filing discusses customary affirmative, negative, and financial covenants for this type of facility.

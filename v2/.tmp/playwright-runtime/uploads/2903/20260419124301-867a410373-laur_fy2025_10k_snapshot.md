Snapshot notes:

- Laureate says it operates private higher-education institutions in Mexico and Peru and serves traditional and non-traditional students through multiple brands, price points, and teaching modalities.
- The company says private providers make up about 39% of the Mexico higher-education market and about 76% of the Peru higher-education market, with public-capacity limits supporting private demand.
- Laureate says demand for online and hybrid learning in Mexico and Peru is increasing and that its target is for 40% to 60% of student credit hours to be taken online over time.
- Laureate reports two operating segments: Mexico and Peru.
- Institution and enrollment snapshot at 2025-12-31:
  - Mexico: UVM 132,200 and UNITEC 137,200, or 269,400 combined.
  - Peru: UPC 78,400, UPN 129,900, and CIBERTEC 20,000, or 228,300 combined.
- Laureate defines enrollment as its lead revenue indicator and notes that Peru's primary intake is in Q1 while Mexico's primary intake is in Q3.
- 2025 segment financial snapshot:
  - Mexico revenue: $877.4 million; adjusted EBITDA: $229.4 million.
  - Peru revenue: $824.4 million; adjusted EBITDA: $328.6 million.

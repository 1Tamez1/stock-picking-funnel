2025 Premium Cataract Surgery Market Report: Analysis of the Top 13 Countries for 2024 to 2030, October, 2025

The “2025 Premium Cataract Surgery Market Report” provides an updated, expanded view of the world’s top PCS markets, providing seven years of historical data and five-year forecasts for PC-IOL, toric IOL, and FLACS procedures across 13 key countries. 

This edition adds new analyses to guide strategic decisions, including PCS promotion strategies, surgeon adoption patterns, and premium IOL selection methodologies, along with post‑LASIK and premium IOL surgery volumes since 2005 to highlight the population driving PCS demand. 

The report also covers market trends such as the recovery of PCS procedures in South Korea and Japan, the impact of new FLACS lasers like the Lensar Ally, demographic drivers including baby boomers fueling refractive lens exchange, and the fastest-growing premium IOL, the RxSight post-op adjustable lens, expanding into Canada, South Korea, and Singapore. 

The “2025 Premium Cataract Surgery Market Report” delivers a clear view of the world’s leading PCS markets, covering 13 countries that together generate 84 percent of global PCS manufacturer revenue. From the United States and China to Japan, Germany, and beyond, each market is analyzed in depth, highlighting key trends, growth drivers, and strategic opportunities. 

The report zeroes in on the factors that matter most for companies evaluating market potential or planning to launch premium cataract surgery devices, providing actionable insights to guide investment and expansion decisions. 

The “2025 Premium Cataract Surgery Market Report” offers a comprehensive look at the global PCS landscape, highlighting key trends, market dynamics, and strategic opportunities for companies and providers. The report combines global insights with deep, country-level analysis to guide decision-making and investment in this rapidly evolving market. 

Key highlights include: 

  • Premium IOLs and technologies: Presbyopia-correcting, toric, intermediate-optimized, and post-op adjustable lenses; FLACS lasers and arcuate incisions; manual limbal relaxing incisions; and excimer IOL enhancements. 
  • Top market profiles: In-depth snapshots and executive summaries for the 13 largest PCS markets—United States, China, Japan, France, Germany, Brazil, Canada, India, Spain, South Korea, Australia, the UK, and Italy. 
  • Market and financial analysis: Country-specific forecasts for PCS procedures, manufacturer revenue, and provider revenue; cost comparisons for public and private cataract surgery options. 
  • Provider and surgeon insights: Breakdown of PCS procedures by cataract-only, cataract/refractive, and refractive-only surgeons in each key market. 
  • Competitive landscape: Manufacturer revenue shares by country and globally, plus a listing of the most important PCS eye hospitals and clinics. 
  • Emerging markets: Overview of the next six most significant PCS countries ranked by market size.

# Morgan Stanley 2026 private credit outlook snapshot

- Publication date: 2025-12-16.
- Investor demand for private credit remained constructive entering 2026.
- A higher-for-longer refinancing and M&A environment can support private-credit lenders.
- Semi-liquid vehicles were described as approaching one-third of the roughly $1 trillion US direct lending market.
